Welcome to "Guess the Number"
Are you up for a thrilling challenge? Test your wits and intuition in our exciting game, "Guess the Number."
Here's how it works:

Rules:

1. We'll pick a secret number from a specific range.
2. Your mission is to guess the correct number.
3. Type in your guesses in the provided input field.
4. After each guess, we'll give you clues to help you figure out if the secret number is higher or lower.
5. Keep guessing until you uncover the secret number or decide to call it a day.
6. Enjoy the suspense and see how fast you can crack the code!
7. Good luck and have a fantastic time playing "Guess the Number"!
